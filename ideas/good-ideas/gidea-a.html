good ideas

  1. Implement a composting program in your community to reduce waste and create fertilizer.
  2. Start a book club to encourage reading and discussion among friends.
  3. Volunteer at a local animal shelter to provide care and support for animals in need.
  4. Organize a beach clean-up to remove trash and debris from the coastline.
  5. Create a community garden to provide fresh produce for those in need and to promote sustainability.
  6. Host a film festival to showcase independent or lesser-known movies and support the arts.
  7. Organize a fundraiser to raise money for a local charity or non-profit organization.
  8. Start a mentorship program to help young people develop skills and achieve their goals.
  9. Create a support group for people facing a particular challenge or struggle, such as mental illness or addiction.
  10. Host a workshop or seminar to teach a skill or provide education on a particular topic.
